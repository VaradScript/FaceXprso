Happy

A happy expression is characterized by smiling, where the corners of the mouth are lifted, and often the eyes are partially closed.

Sad

A sad expression involves a frowning mouth, with the corners of the mouth turned down, and eyebrows that might be drawn together.

Surprise

A surprised expression typically includes raised eyebrows, wide-open eyes, and a mouth that may be open.

Neutral

A neutral expression is where the face shows no specific emotion, maintaining a relaxed and balanced appearance.

Angry

An angry expression typically includes furrowed brows, a glaring look, and lips pressed tightly together or slightly open.

Fearful

A fearful expression often involves widened eyes, raised eyebrows, and a tense mouth, indicating apprehension or anxiety.
